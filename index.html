My Projects

Simulation 1 "The Circulatory System"

This lesson explores how the circulatory, respiratory, and digestive systems work together to transport essential materials for cellular respiration. Students will learn:

  • How oxygen and glucose reach cells from the environment.
  • Why removing carbon dioxide is vital for the body.
  • The roles of the heart and lungs in maintaining cellular function.
  • How changes in heart and lung activity affect overall system performance.

By the end of the lesson, students will be able to predict how increased heart and lung rates influence cellular respiration efficiency.

Simulation 2 "The Electric Circuit"

In this lesson, students will explore the fundamentals of electric circuits through a virtual lab. They will learn:

  • The roles of different components in an electrical circuit.
  • How electricity flows and powers devices.
  • The key principles for building safe and efficient circuits.

By the end of the lesson, students will understand how to design and analyze simple circuits while ensuring safe operation.
