My Projects

Simulation 1 "The Circulatory System"

This lesson explores how the circulatory, respiratory, and digestive systems work together to transport essential materials for cellular respiration. Students will learn:

  • How oxygen and glucose reach cells from the environment.
  • Why removing carbon dioxide is vital for the body.
  • The roles of the heart and lungs in maintaining cellular function.
  • How changes in heart and lung activity affect overall system performance.

By the end of the lesson, students will be able to predict how increased heart and lung rates influence cellular respiration efficiency.

Simulation 2 "The Electric Circuit"

In this lesson, students will explore the fundamentals of electric circuits through a virtual lab. They will learn:

  • The roles of different components in an electrical circuit.
  • How electricity flows and powers devices.
  • The key principles for building safe and efficient circuits.

By the end of the lesson, students will understand how to design and analyze simple circuits while ensuring safe operation.

Simulation 3 "Plant Cell: Structure and Function"

In this lesson, students will explore the structure and function of plant cells. They will learn:

  • The key components of a plant cell, including the cell wall, membrane, cytoplasm, nucleus, and chloroplasts.
  • How each organelle contributes to the survival and growth of plants.
  • The role of vacuoles in maintaining water balance within plant cells.
  • How the process of watering affects cell function and plant health.

By the end of the lesson, students will understand how plant cells respond to environmental changes and the importance of each cell structure in plant growth.

Game "Dog and Bone"

A logic-based game where players build rules using a visual system to reach the objective. Game Features:

  • Design custom rules to guide the player to its goal.
  • Use a drag-and-drop system to create logical sequences.
  • Test different rule combinations to solve puzzles.
  • Progress through multiple levels with increasing difficulty.
  • Engaging visuals and interactive gameplay.

By the end of the game, players will develop problem-solving skills and logical thinking by experimenting with different rule combinations.
